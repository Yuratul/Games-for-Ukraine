This is our future: coding. This is AC623.

Welcome to AC623 (no, this is not Air Canada Flight 623's home page). We offer a multitude of free coding tutorials. We specialize in the three coding languages of the Web (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript).

Why?

Because we share information through the World Wide Web. Originally, the Web was made for scientists at CERN to share information.

We are not scientists, but we are sharing information.

That's what matters.

The web is being pillaged with useless stuff: cat pictures, dumb memes, social media platforms, and annoying youtube videos of kids trying to be famous… the list goes on.
Use your web development skills to do something useful.

For free. Forever. - Khan Academy

We are driven by this statement. Learning should be universal: all intelligence can be shared.

Computers are expensive enough. We don't think it is ethical to pay for a computer and pay to learn how to code with it. That just makes no sense.
